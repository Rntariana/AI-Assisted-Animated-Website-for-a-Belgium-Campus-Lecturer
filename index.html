Mr. Ikraam Sadek
// JUNIOR LECTURER — PROGRAMMING DEPT.

Mr. Sadek is a dynamic and engaging Junior Lecturer at Belgium Campus iTversity, holding an Advanced Diploma in Application Development from Rosebank College. He teaches across multiple programming and information systems modules, from first-year fundamentals to second-year advanced topics.

His classrooms are built on discussion, interaction, and real-world application. He believes learning happens best when students are active participants — not passive listeners.
